Generate recovery seed

This screen will generate your recovery seed, which is 12 words that will be used to create your master private key. These 12 words can be used to recover your wallet in the event that you lose your password or don't have access to your wallet. Your 12 word recovery phase cannot ever be recreated or restored.

The entropy (randomness) of your recovery phrase will be generated from your password, the current time and the picture that you draw below.
